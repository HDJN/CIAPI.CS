

trigger a buy when a market drops below a specified value

   advanced strategy: enter a 'staging' mode when market drops below specified value and wait until it stops dropping to make the trade.
                      determining when this is could involve allowing a small degree of up/down fluctuation to a specific range.


                      
trigger a sale when the open position rises above a specified value

   advanced strategy: enter a 'staging' mode when the open position rises above a certain value and wait until it stops rising to sell.
                      determining when this is could involve allowing a small degree of up/down fluctuation to a specific range.
